Terms & Conditions
  • Overweight charges: $100/ton for Concrete bins; $130/ton for Recycling, Source Separated, Non-Recyclable, or Demolition bins over stated limits.
  • Metal-Only bins are not charged overweight fees and are credited based on market value minus transportation.
  • Driver standby fee of $150/hr applies if bin is not ready when dispatched (waived for delays under 20 minutes). After-hours service requests subject to additional charges.
  • Commingled Recycling bins must contain at least 65% recyclable material by weight and volume. Single-Item bins must contain 95%+ of a single item or will be reclassified.
  • Hazardous/Universal Solid Waste not accepted.
  • FIC fee (Facility Incoming Material Charge, currently $35.00) included on each invoice per service.
  • Prices are valid for 30 days and are for this project only. After 30 days, pricing may be subject to change. Tax rates and jurisdiction fees may change without notice.
